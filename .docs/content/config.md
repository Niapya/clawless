Configuring **ClawLess** is simple.

Just open the **web control panel**, then configure:

- Your **Provider**.
- Your **Language Model**, Context Limit and Output Limit.
- Your **Embedding Model**.

Or you can paste your configured JSON into it.

And that’s it — you’re ready to go. ✅

You can also specify a custom **Language Model** for a **subAgent** in the **Agent** settings section.
This allows your **Claw** to delegate subtasks to different subAgents.

If you want to add additional capabilities, such as:

- 🌐 **Browser access**(e.g. Broswer Use, Kernel)
- 🔎 **Web search**(e.g. Tavily)

You can simply add an **MCP**.

> *why don’t we just build these features in by default?*
>
> Well… mainly because **no one has paid us any advertising fees**.  
> Also, there isn’t really a **unified interface** for these services yet—so keeping them modular through MCP is much cleaner.
